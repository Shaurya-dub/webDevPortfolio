    A basic little art quiz that tests your knowledge on all things art ranging from Picasso to Murakami, and provides you with your score on completion. This app uses vanilla JavaScript to interact with and manipulate the DOM for it's functionality. I thoroughly enjoyed making this as it allowed me to express my passion for art.

  • Brew-Bud

    Brew-Bud was created to help my friends and I locate good breweries for when we visit the U.S.A. This app makes API calls to help find breweries near you, wherever you are in the United States. Results can be filtered by brewery name, city, state, and (thanks to a great geocoding API) zip-code.

  • Pour Decisions

    I made Pour Decisions so that my friends and family would stop bugging me for cocktail recipes. This app was my first foray into jquery, and APIs. It helps you find recipes for cocktails based on drink name, ingredient, or generates a random drink recipe for when you just need one quick (been there, no judgements).

    An App that allows you to search for gifs that relate to your mood, and then add them to an expanding timeline along with the date to chronicle your mood over time.
